VLADIMIR KLASSOVSKY

1817-1877

Born in 1815, he was  “a teacher and writer, from the nobility of the province of Tambov, a graduate of Moscow University, was a teacher of Russian and Latin languages ​​in different schools, then a mentor and an observer of Russian language at the military schools, the inspector class St. Petersburg.” He died in 1877.

POMPEII AND ITS ANTIQUITIES, WITH THE OVERVIEW OF VESUVIUS AND HERCULANUM

Systematic Description of Pompeii and of Antiquities Discovered There, with the Overview of Vesuvius and Herculanum by Vladimir Klassovsky was first published in Saint Petersburg in 1848, with 4 editions, 4th edition in 1883). Its later title was Pompeii and Its Antiquities, with the Overview of Vesuvius and Herculanum.
